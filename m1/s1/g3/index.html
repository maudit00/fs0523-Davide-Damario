Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart, cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holyday starter!

Image : Sally Vegas

fresh cranberry recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

    For the focaccia
  • 4 cups (500gr) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350gr) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan
    For the topping
  • 3 mediums yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing top
  • 10 ounches brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a pairing knife if desired, though it's not necessary
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the foaccia dough
    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix toghjeter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, tough I sometimes knead it a gew times in the bowl when mixing bu hand to incorporate the last of the flour
